Project.

Grupo Chapital.



Identity + Branding + Web design for the biggest marketing of electric automotive parts of the Mexican southwest region.

Grupo Chapital came to Le Brand with the desire to strengthen the image of their already well-known 4th generation family’s company name. Grupo Chapital wanted to establish a unique identity for their company and establish a presence in the digital world. As with many other businesses in Mexico, there have been few resources invested in the corporate image, marketing, and communication aspects of this company. Grupo Chapital reached out to Le Brand in search of new branding that could represent all of the company’s essential motives and desires, in order to foster expansion and allow the company to stand out in competitive regional markets.

Le Brand also had the opportunity to create an array of diverse applications of the new branding that will be in direct contact with the customer. This branding will reinforce the motives and desires of Grupo Chapital. The new branding has been very well received by users, due to the fact that it is visually attractive andaccurately describes Grupo Chapital’s experience and expertise.

Empowering visual capital.
