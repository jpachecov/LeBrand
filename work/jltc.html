JLTC Partners

Identity + Branding + Web Design for JLTC Holding, with a variety of financial companies but it mainly specializes in: Agro-business, Financial investment and real state.
JLTC holding is a financial company that possesses a large amount of capital and many years of experience. JLTC enlisted the help of Le Brand in creating a new image in order to communicate the company’s strengths and professionalism to their future investors and financial associates. Le Brand did this by creating a very detailed branding that completely went above and beyond the client’s expectations.
The new image was a great success with the businessman and new investors. JLTC was so happy with the results that they asked Le Brand to continue working on their website and their social media networks.
