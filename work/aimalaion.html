Project.

Centennial Organics.

Identity + Branding + Web design for a studio and record producer in Cholula, Puebla.

Aimalaion is a Mexican recording studio established in 2013 by a musician and audio engineer from the city of Puebla who has many years of experience in themusic recording industry. In order to develop Aimalaion, he decided to create a new branding design to reflect the personality of his vision as well as his passion for the projects and collaborations the studio would offer. Le Brand designed the studio’s identity and branding, emphasizing the professionalism, high quality, andinnovation of the new studio as well as the artist’s personal interests, such as tattoos and new sounds.

The logo that Le Brand has created for Aimalaion centers on an illustration of a lion. The design includes a fluorescent spectrum of colors that received very positive feedback from the team at Aimalaion as well as some local musicians.
