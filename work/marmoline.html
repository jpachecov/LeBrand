Project.

Marmoline.

Identity + Branding + Web Design for one of the most important marble companies inn the Mexico’s southeastern region. The company extracts, distributes and sells out marble pieces for architectonic purposes.

Marmoline contacted Le Brand in search of an urgent re-design in order to consolidate their identity and make their company a more attractive option for customers.

After conducting marketing research on all the different process that the company was involved with, Le Brand created new branding that reflected the characteristics that the company wished to communicate to the users, such as elegance, quality, excellent design, and sobriety.
