Project.

CMX.

Identity + Branding + Web Design for the project Conectando México foundation, with the objective of giving free Internet educational purpose access for specific marginal communities in Mexico city.

The Conectando Mexico foundation’s objective is to raise money in order to grant free internet access to marginalized and targeted areas of Mexico City. They hope to give thousands of people the chance to access the vast information, education, and training resources the Internet has to offer. The Conectando Mexico, with the help of other educational organizations, hopes to use the Internet to enrich the education of thousands of people. Le Brand had the incredible opportunity to help with this project by creating the branding, web design, and promotional applications needed by the CMX project.
